My Life After Queen's

Part 1: Finding a job

The first thing I plan on doing after my time at Queen's is finished, would be to find a job. This job will almost certainly be in the Computing field, as that is what I am passionate about and qualified for. Once I have found a job, which in my experiance shouldn't be too difficult based on my prior experiance with finding jobs, I will use this job to further develop my skills in the field of computing. Once I'm satisfied with the skills I have developped, and have saved enough money, I would like to go back to school for a graduate degree in Computer Science.

Part 2: Grad school

While my time at Queen's may be long gone at this point, I would still like to further my education in computing. This would be for my own personal knowledge, as well as for my future career. I am aware that computer science jobs don't pay as much as project managers, which is why after a graduate degree in computer science, I would like to go back to school for an MBA. This will allow me to pursue managerial roles with much more success then otherwise, which would allow me to make a lot more money then simply programming, however I would still like to stay in the field of computer science.
